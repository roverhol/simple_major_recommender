# Part 3: Version C Report: Results, Ethics, and Transparency

In Part 3, you will submit a short report about your Version C recommender.

This report should build directly on your Part 2 draft data-collection write-up and your Version C evaluation results.

## Purpose

Part 3 is where you explain not only what changed and how it performed, but also why your data choices and transparency practices are reasonable.

## Required Report Sections

Your report should include Sections 1-3 below.

### 1) Version C data collection (from Part 2 draft)

Use and refine your Part 2 draft. Briefly explain:

- Where the new data came from (URL(s) or dataset name)
- How you obtained it
- How you linked it to Cal Poly Humboldt majors
- Enough detail so another student could reproduce your data and mapping process

### 2) Version C changes and evaluation results

Summarize:

- Your one major data change (rationale and expected impact)
- Your two small algorithm/parameter changes (what you changed and why)
- Results from evaluating Version C from part 2.

When possible, compare A vs B vs C clearly so trade-offs are visible.

### 3) Ethical and transparency reflection

Return to the transparency/responsibility work from Lab XX and explain:

- Why your Version C data choices are reasonable
- What trade-offs your approach makes 
- How your recommender communicates transparency to users
  - what data is used,
  - how recommendations are generated,
  - key limitations users should know


## Required Visual Audit Artifact

Include at least one visual artifact that helps make your recommender explainable.

This can be included directly in your report or as a clearly labeled appendix figure.
