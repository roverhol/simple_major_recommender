# Lecture activity: Class selection of evaluation metrics

## Purpose

Convert team-proposed metrics from Lab 6 (where each team proposes a structural metric, a human-centered metric, and an exposure or ethical metric, with definitions and justifications) into one shared evaluation contract for all students. This activity should occur after that lab and before Part 1 of the major recommender project (when students compare Versions A, B, and later C in Part 2).

## Inputs

- Team submissions containing proposed:
  - structural metric,
  - human-centered metric,
  - exposure/ethical metric,
  with definitions and justifications.

## In-class consensus process

1. Teams present or summarize their metric choices and reasoning.
2. Cross-team discussion compares feasibility, fairness, interpretability, and alignment with transparency goals.
3. Class negotiates trade-offs and selects one final metric set.
4. Instructor confirms final definitions and reporting format.

## Output (evaluation contract)

- One shared metric set used by all students in Part 1 Versions A, B, and C.
- Shared understanding of what counts as evidence of improvement.
