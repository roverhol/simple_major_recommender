# Lab 3: Hierarchical clustering and dendrograms — learning outcomes

## Learning Outcomes

- Constructing dendrograms from a distance/disimilarity matrix.
- Computing linkage distances for given clusters (single, complete, average, centroid, ward).
- Identifying impactful choices (distance/disimilarity metric, scaling, linkage, ties).
- Using `AgglomerativeClustering` and `compute_linkage()` to produce dendrograms.
- Cutting dendrograms to form clusters (`cut_tree`).
