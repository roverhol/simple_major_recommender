# DATA 322 Lab 5: Study Group Recommender Work Session

## Purpose

Lab 5 extends Lab 4 by giving students dedicated time to implement, debug, and polish the study group recommender artifacts. Most importantly, teams have time to communicate with each other.

## Likely Focus Areas

- Finalize data cleaning and feature encoding choices.
- Refine similarity definition and clustering parameters.
- Validate dendrogram cuts and resulting group assignments.
- Improve communication artifacts (slides, testing-day flow, and interpretation notes).
- Prepare final deliverables for submission and testing day.
